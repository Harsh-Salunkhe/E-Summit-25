About Us

Entrepreneurship Cell NIT Bhopal is a voluntary organisation aimed at fostering a vibrant startup culture and nurturing a comprehensive entrepreneurial ecosystem among the youth.

Every year, E-Cell organises Central India's largest entrepreneurial fest, E-Summit along with its flagship podcast series, Pe-Charcha, bringing together a diverse community of budding entrepreneurs, innovators, and creators from across the nation. E-Summit offers an unparalleled platform for learning, networking, and showcasing talent through engaging activities like the Startup Expo, Business Plan Competition, IPL Auction and more. It serves as a catalyst for creativity, collaboration, and the growth of the next generation of entrepreneurs. E-Summit provides a dynamic stage where ideas are transformed into opportunities and innovative visions into successful ventures.

OUR CO-TITLE SPONSOR

Steel Authority of India Limited (SAIL) stands as a hallmark of excellence in the nation's steel industry, driving India's progress for decades. From its headquarters in New Delhi, SAIL has consistently delivered high quality steel products that form the foundation of the country's infrastructure and industrial achievements. Its legacy is defined by resilience, innovation, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Guided by a vision of sustainable development, SAIL integrates cutting-edge technology with environmentally responsible practices to set new benchmarks in the steel industry. Beyond its industrial achievements, the company remains deeply committed to empowering communities through impactful initiatives in education, healthcare, and skill enhancement. At its core, SAIL represents trust, ethics, and innovation, working to shape a sustainable and prosperous future for the nation.
